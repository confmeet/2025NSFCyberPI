About

The 2025 National Science Foundation Cyber Principal Investigator Meeting, or 2025 NSF Cyber PI Meeting, brings together PIs and senior personnel from multiple NSF programs: Cyberinfrastructure for Sustained Scientific Innovation (CSSI), Training-based Workforce Development for Advanced Cyberinfrastructure (CyberTraining), and Strengthening the Cyberinfrastructure Professionals Ecosystem (SCIPE).

We also encourage colleagues funded by the Campus Cyberinfrastructure (CC*), other Office of Advanced Cyberinfrastructure (OAC) programs, and Computational Science and Data-enabled Engineering (CDS&E) to join the event (up to our capacity).
